Important information

Contraindications for InfraShape sessions

Before booking, please carefully review the list below. If you are unsure whether the treatment is suitable for you, please don't hesitate to contact us.

The device cannot be used in the following cases:

  • During pregnancy
  • While breastfeeding
  • After surgery
  • During an acute infection and immediately afterward
  • In the case of respiratory illness
  • In the case of fever
  • In the case of cardiovascular disease
  • With thrombosis or a tendency to thrombosis
  • With vascular problems
  • In the case of untreated high blood pressure
  • With an inguinal hernia
  • During heavy menstruation
  • With gynaecological problems — e.g. endometriosis
  • NO USE OF PAPRIKA OR CHILLI CREAMS
